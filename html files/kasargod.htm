kasargod

The district is bordered to the south by Kerala's Kannur district, and to the southeast and north by Karnataka's districts of Kodagu and Dakshina Kannada respectively. To the east, the district is bordered by the Western Ghats, while along the west, it is bordered by the Laccadive Sea. The district, covering an area of around 1992 km2, has a population (2011 census) of 1,307,375. It has four taluks, namely, Kasaragod, Hosdurg, Vellarikundu and Manjeshwaram Taluk. It has three municipalities: Kasaragod, Kanhangad, Nileshwaram and 38 Gram panchayats.
